A bit about me

I'm a Cornell M.Eng. student in Mechanical Engineering with dual B.Sc. degrees from Penn State in Biomedical and Mechanical Engineering. My work sits at the intersection of quality systems, process engineering, and hands-on validation — building the frameworks that make products safe, compliant, and manufacturable.

At Fresenius Kabi, I supported 510(k) submissions, performed root cause analysis on device complaints, revised 40+ technical drawings for FDA and ASME compliance, and engineered automated test rigs that cut validation labor by ~28 hours per run. I work within ISO 13485 and ISO 9001 quality management systems and bring that same rigor — precise, standards-driven, detail-obsessed — to every project.

I'm based in Ithaca, NY and open to relocation anywhere for the right opportunity.
